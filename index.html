Asteroids

WASD or Arrow keys to move
Press 'r' for hyperspace
Press 'e' to stop
Press 'z' for Pierce Shot (Unlocks at Score 2000+
Press 'v' for Multishot (Unlocks at Score 5000+)
Other info:
Asteroids are worth 200 points
Touching one removes 1000 points
Pierce shot has a 1 second cooldown
Multishot has a ~5 second cooldown
