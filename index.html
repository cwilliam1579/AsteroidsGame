Asteroids

Press 'w' or ↑ to accelerate
Press 's' or ↓ to decelerate
Press 'a' or ← to rotate counter clockwise
Press 'd' or → to rotate clockwise
Press 'r' to activate hyperspace
Press 'e' to stop
An Asteroid is worth 200 points
Touching an Asteroid takes away 1000 points
Reach 2000 points to unlock Pierce Shot, which can only be used when stationary.
